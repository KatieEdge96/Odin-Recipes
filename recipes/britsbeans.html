British Beans on Toast

Beans on toast have been eaten for years everyday in the UK for breakfast, lunch and dinner. It’s a super simple dish, but it’s comfort food on a plate for many of us.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tin Heinz baked beans
  • 2 slices of bread (choose your favourite)
  • salt and pepper to season

METHOD

  1. Put the Heinz beans into a small pot over medium low heat
  2. Put the bread in the toaster, and make it as desired. (You can also butter the toast if you wish)
  3. Stir the beans and head until almost boiling, but don't allow it to boil
  4. Place the toast onto the plate or plates, and spoon your beans over the top of each slice
  5. Add some grated cheese ontop for extra flavour (it isn't necessary).
